Planning is Essential

Draw out your yard and work out the best/easiest way to run your wire to form your new containment area. Simple is better and use existing fencing wherever possible.

You do not need to bury the wire! With an electric dog fence system, attaching to existing fences with cable ties or U-shaped nails is fine.

Complete a Loop

You need to run the wire from your transmitter and get back to it again (a loop). There are lots of ways to do this that suits a variety of different scenarios for every yard or property.
