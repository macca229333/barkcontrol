Indoor Pet Barriers

Indoor barrier zones are an effective solution for keeping your pets away from certain areas around the house. Benefits of indoor containment zones include:

  • Keeps pets off furniture and benches
  • Limits their access to certain rooms such as child nurseries and kitchens
  • Keeps your other indoor pets (guinea pigs, birds, fish) safe.
  • Easy to set up and use
  • Suitable for dogs and cats
