Electric Bark Control Collars

Control your dog's nuisance barking. Static electric bark collars instantly deliver a safe static stimulation to your dog when their barking is detected. Bark collar models also available for howling/whining dogs. Available in models for tiny to giant dogs.

Dog Containment Fence Systems

Keep your dog safe in your yard. Dog containment fences are the most reliable form of dog containment because there's no way your dog can dig, jump or pass through boundary. Cover up to 150 acres and add an unlimited number of dogs to your system.

Electric Remote Training Collars

Train your dog out of disobedient behaviours, refine training commands or communicate with your dog in working conditions. Options for close range use and up to 1600 meters. Static electric remote training collars keep you in control in every situation.
