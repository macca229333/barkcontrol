So What are the Types of Bark Collars?

... and What Type is Best For My Dog? Here's What You Should Consider:

Electric Bark Collars

Electric or 'static' bark collars are the most effective type of barking collar. They administer a harmless static pulse to your dog when barking is detected. They have a 99% success rate if you choose the right one for your dog. Simply... They Work!

If you have a working breed dog, an electric anti-bark collar is going to be your best option. All of our static barking collars have a 30-day Money Back Performance Guarantee. What do you have to lose?

Citronella Spray Bark Collars

Citronella barking collars administer a harmless citronella spray to your dogs snout when they bark. They are the next most effective after electric collars with around a 75% success rate. This is higher in pet breeds and lower in working breeds.

We offer a 14 day performance guarantee on all of our citronella bark collars. Buy it, try it and if doesn’t work send it back!

Vibration and Ultrasonic Bark Collars

These are the least successful types of barking collars and we only recommend to young pups or very timid dogs. They offer a lower correction than other types of bark collars and many dogs will ignore them after a period of time.

Ultrasonic collars and devices also correct all dogs in hearing range. So are not recommended in multi dog situations.
