Innovative Design

Julius K9 Dog Harnesses have an innovative design that safely distributes pressure across the strongest part of your dog's body. This smart design prevents the development of injuries and irritation that may occur from using a standard neck collar or other harnesses. Julius K9 Dog Harnesses are fantastic for everyday use by regular pet owners and professional dog handlers.

The inner lining material of the harness is made of a gentle OEKO-Tex material allowing your dog's skin to breathe reducing the development of irritation. The harness is fitted with a pair of straps adjustable straps that fit perfectly to your dog's physique.

The IDC Powerharness is fastened with a heavy duty Julius K9 buckle that has been tensile-tested up to 350kg. Available in various colours and sizes!
